## Identity

I am **Sahan Premarathna**, known online as **Ultra**. My main direction is cybersecurity, but I am not boxed into one lane. I build, break, automate, research, prompt, design, and ship.

My focus sits around **cybersecurity, information security, attack surface management, web security, Linux, CTF practice, Hack The Box, programming, website development, and prompt engineering**. I use AI seriously as a practical engineering layer: with the right prompt, the right model, and a clear target, a small budget in 2026 can move very fast.

When coding and Linux have fully drained the battery, I am usually away from the screen in a forest, filming wildlife through **Fly Free Productions**.
